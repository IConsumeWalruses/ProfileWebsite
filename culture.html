Culture



I come from the chinese culture. We have a variety of things we are known for, one notably being the holidays. These holidays can include Chinese New Year, Dragon Boat Festival and Mid Autumn Festival, also known as the Mooncake Festival. In Chinese New Year, we (actually, the parents) pass around red pockets which contain money. In the Dragon boat festival, some people scatter rice in the water to feed the fish, according to an chinese legend. We also eat zongzi (a type of sticky rice) on that day. In the Mid Autumn Festival, we eat and gift to others mooncakes, in honour of another chinese legend.
